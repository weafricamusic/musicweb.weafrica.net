WeAfrica Music Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WeAfrica Music collects, uses, and protects information when you use the WeAfrica Music mobile and web applications.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, and profile details.
  • User content you upload, including audio, images, and metadata.
  • Device and technical information such as IP address, device type, app version, and usage logs.
  • Engagement data, including likes, follows, playback activity, and in-app interactions.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve WeAfrica Music services.
  • Personalize content recommendations and user experience.
  • Enable social and creator features, including uploads and live experiences.
  • Support moderation, fraud prevention, and platform safety.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers that help operate the app (for example hosting, analytics, cloud storage, and authentication providers), with legal authorities when required by law, and with other users according to your profile/content visibility settings.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • You can update or correct certain account information in the app.
  • You can request account deletion and related data deletion where applicable. Visit Delete Your Account for instructions.
  • You can manage notification preferences in your device and app settings.

Children's Privacy

WeAfrica Music is not intended for children under the age required by applicable local law. If we learn we collected personal data from a child without proper consent, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and revise the effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@weafrica.net.
