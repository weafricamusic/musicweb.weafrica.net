Delete Your WeAfrica Music Account

Effective date: April 3, 2026

WeAfrica Music allows users to permanently delete their account and associated personal data.

Option 1: Delete directly in the app (recommended)

  1. Open WeAfrica Music and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete account.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm and submit.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send a request from your registered email address to privacy@weafrica.net.

What is deleted

  • Your profile and account identifiers.
  • Your uploaded content and related metadata, where applicable.
  • Associated app activity data tied to your account.

What may be retained

  • Certain records may be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or financial compliance obligations.
  • Backup copies may persist for a limited period before secure deletion.

Support

For deletion support or privacy questions, contact privacy@weafrica.net.
