# WeAfrica Music Plan Blueprint

Status: product blueprint (consumer + creator + ticketing)
Date: 2026-03-19

## 1. Consumer Structure

### Free (MWK 0 / month)
- Ads every about 2 songs
- Limited skips (target: 6 per hour)
- No background play
- No downloads
- Standard audio quality
- Limited gifts (send only)
- Watch-only access to live battles
- Song requests disabled
- No VIP badge
- No bonus coins
- Can buy standard event tickets only
- Standard feed and discovery

### Premium (MWK 4,000 / month)
- No ads
- Unlimited skips
- Background play
- Audio downloads
- High-quality audio
- Standard gifts
- Full live battle access (watch and interact)
- Song requests disabled
- No VIP badge
- No monthly bonus coins
- Limited early access to some content
- Can buy standard and selected VIP tickets
- Standard feed placement

### Platinum (MWK 8,500 / month)
- Everything in Premium
- Video downloads
- VIP gifts
- Priority live battle access
- Song requests enabled
- Highlighted comments
- Exclusive and early content access
- VIP badge
- 200 monthly bonus coins
- Priority feed placement and top discovery
- Can redeem bonus coins toward tickets
- Priority booking for VIP events

## 2. Artist Structure

### Free (MWK 0 / month)
- Upload up to 5 songs per month
- Basic analytics (plays, likes)
- Watch-only live battle access
- Cannot create or host battles
- No monetization
- No VIP badge
- No bonus coins
- Standard feed only
- Cannot sell tickets
- Limited fan engagement (comments only)

### Premium (MWK 6,000 / month)
- Upload up to 20 songs per month
- Detailed analytics (audience trends)
- Participate in standard live battles
- Cannot create VIP or priority battles
- Limited battle promotion
- Standard feed placement
- Fan engagement: standard gifts and partial coin earnings
- No VIP badge
- Limited early access to some content
- Moderate monetization
- Can sell tickets for standard events
- Song requests disabled

### Platinum (MWK 12,500 / month)
- Unlimited uploads
- Full analytics dashboard (plays, engagement, fan trends)
- Create and schedule VIP or priority battles
- Priority battle access and promotion
- Participate in VIP battles
- Priority feed placement
- Full monetization: coins, VIP gifts, ad revenue
- VIP badge
- Song requests enabled in live battles
- Highlighted comments
- Early and exclusive content access
- 200 monthly bonus coins
- Can sell tickets for VIP concerts, events, or battles
- Exclusive battle and event invites
- Maximum fan engagement and promotion

## 3. DJ Structure

### Free (MWK 0 / month)
- Cannot create or host battles
- Basic analytics (viewers, watch time)
- Watch-only live battles
- No VIP badge
- No monetization
- No ticket hosting
- Limited audience interaction

### Premium (MWK 8,000 / month)
- Can host standard battles
- Moderate analytics (audience demographics, engagement)
- Limited battle promotion
- Fan engagement: standard gifts and partial coin earnings
- No VIP badge
- Limited song-request priority
- Can host standard paid concerts or events
- Limited early access to some content

### Platinum (MWK 15,000 / month)
- Create and schedule VIP or priority live battles
- Host VIP battles with full fan interaction controls
- Full analytics dashboard (real-time engagement, top fans, battle stats)
- Priority battle and event promotion
- Full monetization: coins, VIP gifts, ad revenue share
- VIP badge
- Full controls for song requests, polls, highlighted comments
- Can host VIP concerts, events, and ticketed battles
- Early and exclusive content release
- 200 monthly bonus coins
- Exclusive event hosting rights
- Maximum audience engagement and visibility

## 4. Ticketing and Events Structure

### Buying Tickets (Consumers)
- Free: standard events only
- Premium: standard and selected VIP events
- Platinum: full access with early access, priority booking, and bonus coin redemption

### Selling Tickets (Artists and DJs)
- Free: cannot sell tickets
- Premium: standard events only
- Platinum: VIP and priority events with full promotion and dashboards

### Event Types
- Free audience mode: watch-only
- Premium: standard concerts and battles
- Platinum: VIP concerts, battles, and exclusive events

### Revenue Flow
- Free: no ticket monetization
- Premium sellers: partial monetization
- Platinum sellers: full monetization (coins, gifts, ad revenue, tickets)
- Platform commission applies

### Fan Engagement by Tier
- Free: comments only
- Premium: standard gifts and limited coin interactions
- Platinum: VIP gifts, song requests, polls, highlighted comments

### Promotion and Discovery
- Free: standard feed
- Premium: limited feature placement
- Platinum: priority feed and spotlight promotion

## Monetization Engine Summary
- Ads on free users
- Subscriptions (Premium + Platinum)
- Coin top-ups (microtransactions)
- Live interactions (gifts, requests, VIP controls)
- Tickets and events

## Positioning Summary
- Free: drives discovery and FOMO
- Premium: drives broad paid conversion (freedom tier)
- Platinum: drives high-ARPU engagement (status and control tier)
